What is Food Waste?

Food waste is the silent symphony of unfulfilled flavors, a culinary tragedy where ingredients intended for a grand feast end up as discarded notes in the compost bin, leaving the kitchen stage without an encore. It's the untold story of missed gastronomic opportunities, where the potential for delightful dishes transforms into a script of decay, leaving behind a narrative of squandered sustenance.

What is food waste management system?

A Food Waste Management System is like a superhero for our planet's pantry. It's a set of strategies and technologies that rescue surplus food, transform kitchen scraps into valuable resources, and teach everyone to be food waste warriors. By reducing waste, we're not just saving food – we're saving the environment and spreading good vibes. It's a tasty journey from farm to fork to compost bin, making sure no bite goes to waste!

How can we manage food waste?

Here are few steps which we can follow in order to minimize food wastage:
1) Reduce food waste at home by buying only what you need, planning meals, and storing food properly.
2) Compost food scraps to create nutrient-rich soil for plants.
3) Donate extra food to food banks, shelters, and other organizations.
4) Recycle food packaging and containers.
5) Use leftovers creatively in new meals and recipes.
6) Contact your local government to find out what food waste programs are available in your area.
